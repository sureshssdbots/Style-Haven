How to Measure

  • Chest — Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.
  • Length — Measure from the highest point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.
  • Sleeve — Measure from the top of the shoulder to the wrist with arm slightly bent.

When in between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit or sizing down for a slim fit.

Care Instructions

Proper care ensures your Style Haven garments retain their shape, colour, and feel wash after wash. Follow these guidelines to get the most from your investment.

T-Shirts & Polos

  • Machine wash cold (30°C) with similar colours
  • Do not use bleach or fabric softeners
  • Tumble dry on low heat or air dry flat
  • Iron on medium heat — do not iron directly over prints

Hoodies & Sweatshirts

  • Machine wash cold inside out to preserve colour
  • Wash separately for the first 2–3 washes
  • Air dry only — tumble drying may cause shrinkage
  • Do not iron drawstrings or embellishments

Jackets & Outerwear

  • Spot clean with a damp cloth when possible
  • For full wash, use cold water on gentle cycle
  • Hang dry only — lay flat if garment is heavy
  • Store on broad-shouldered hangers to maintain shape
  • Do not dry clean unless label specifically recommends

General Tips

Turn all garments inside out before washing to minimise pilling and colour fade. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Fold heavy knits instead of hanging to prevent stretching.

Our Story

Style Haven was born out of a simple frustration: why should you have to choose between quality and affordability? In 2021, our founders set out to create a menswear brand that refused to compromise.

Starting from a small workshop in Mumbai, we spent two years perfecting our first collection before launching publicly. Every thread was chosen deliberately. Every fit was tested obsessively. Every price point was set with one goal in mind — making premium menswear accessible to every Indian gentleman.

Our Mission

To craft garments that outlast trends, outlast seasons, and outlast the fast-fashion cycle. We believe great clothing should be an investment — in yourself, in your confidence, and in a more sustainable future.

Where We Are Today

What started as a team of three now employs over 50 skilled artisans and craftspeople. We've grown from a single product line to a full menswear catalog — and we're just getting started.

Every stitch is still done with the same care as day one. That will never change.

Craftsmanship

At Style Haven, craftsmanship is not a marketing term — it is the foundation of everything we do. We believe the difference between a good garment and a great one lies entirely in the details most people never notice.

Our Process

  • Fabric Selection — We source directly from certified mills, inspecting each bolt for consistency, weight, and feel
  • Pattern Making — Our in-house designers create patterns by hand, refined through dozens of fit tests
  • Cutting — All pieces are cut with precision to minimise waste and ensure consistent sizing
  • Stitching — A team of 30+ skilled tailors work on each garment, with every seam checked by a quality supervisor
  • Finishing — Every garment is pressed, inspected, and folded by hand before packaging

47 Quality Checks

Before any Style Haven garment leaves our facility, it passes through 47 individual quality checkpoints. From thread tension to button alignment, nothing escapes our scrutiny. Only garments that pass every check earn the Style Haven label.

Fade-Resistant Technology

All our dyed garments use a proprietary colour-lock process developed in partnership with our fabric suppliers. Your Style Haven piece will retain its colour through hundreds of washes — guaranteed.
